Government Decisions

On this page, you will get information regarding most recent Government Resolutions and CabinetDecisions. A Government Resolution is an approval order sanctioning release of funds for development related works. You can check Department-wise Resolutions on this page. You can scroll down the list or select department, date, keyword or unique code for desired Resolution.
