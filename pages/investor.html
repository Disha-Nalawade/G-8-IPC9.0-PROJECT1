Investors page

Maharashtra wholeheartedly welcomes investors from across the globe.

The Foreign Direct Investment - FDI inflows in Maharashtra during April 2000 to September 2022 was Rs.10, 88,502 crore, which was 28.5% of total FDI inflows at All - India level. During June 2020 to December 2022, Maharashtra has attracted investment proposals of Rs. 2.74 lakh crore, with 4.27 lakh expected employment under Magnetic Maharashtra 2.0. (Reference - Economic Survey 2022-23). As such, the state is keen to welcome investors in any and all industries – from agriculture to aerospace. All needed information and points of contact are available on the website of the Maharashtra Industry, Trade, and Investment Facilitation Cell (MAITRI), which is a Single Window Clearance System for investors. It is highly recommended that a potential investor visit these informative pages.

Investors can also write to officials directly from this site, or initiate the process of exploring investment by filling out a simple form. Want to contact an official to guide you through? Indian investors may contact the Superintending Industries Officer, Director of Industries at maitri-mh@gov.in or call at +9122-22622362. International Investors can get all approvals from a Single Window Clearance office in New Delhi.

Maharashtra has a strong history of entrepreneurship, a highly skilled workforce, and best in India transportation infrastructure including new expressways, modernizing rail, and international airports at Mumbai, Pune, and Nagpur.
